About Us


Donate Food

At TSF, we believe that everyone deserves access to nutritious food. Our Food Donation program aims to address food insecurity and make a positive impact in our community. By donating food to those in need, you can contribute to fighting hunger and ensuring that individuals and families have access to wholesome meals. Through our extensive network of partner organizations, we distribute donated food to local shelters, food banks, community centers, and other charitable initiatives.


Donate New Or Used Clothes

At TSF, we believe in the power of clothing to transform lives and make a positive impact. Our Clothes Donation program aims to provide clothing to individuals and families in need, offering them warmth, comfort, and dignity. By donating your gently used or new clothes, you can help support those facing challenging circumstances, such as homelessness, poverty, or natural disasters.

  • Empower Others: Your clothing donation can empower individuals by giving them access to clean, appropriate clothing. It allows them to feel more confident and better equipped to face daily challenges.
  • Sustainability: By donating clothes, you contribute to reducing textile waste and promote environmental sustainability. Instead of discarding garments, you give them a second life, reducing the strain on our planet's resources.
  • Community Support: Your donation supports local communities by providing clothing to those who may not have the means to purchase new items. It fosters a sense of solidarity and helps build a stronger, more caring community.
